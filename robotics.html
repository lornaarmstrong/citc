WHAT IS ROBOTICS?

Robotics is a field combining Engineering and Computer Science. Robots can perform tasks which can be very useful, and can replace humans in doing the task. There are lots of interesting ethical questions about robotics, and so many examples of cool projects, research and products. In robotics, we develop machines for different environments, tasks and with different abilities.

Robots can help solve complex problems and make tasks that humans need to do a lot easier. Examples of assistive robots include medical robots, robots that work in industry to perform key labour tasks, drones and even domestic robots.
