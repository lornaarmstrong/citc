WHAT IS HUMAN COMPUTER INTERACTION?

Human Computer Interaction is an area of Computer Science that focuses on how people use technology. It's all about the user, and really focuses on understanding people, their behaviour and their actions.

Understanding how users navigate through a website or use an app is a great example of Human Computer Interaction research. You can gather groups of users and interview them learn more about their opinions and experience in focus groups. You can develop tools and techniques to make sure systems are built suitably for the users. You can become an expert in accessibility and usability. Analysing user interfaces, summarising user groups and having a good knowledge of what makes users different is key. In HCI, there are lots of very interesting things to explore and to do!
